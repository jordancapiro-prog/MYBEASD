FAQ

Answers to common questions about MemoryMatch Pro.

Is Memory Match Master mobile-friendly?

Yes. The game is fully responsive and designed to work perfectly on desktops, tablets, and smartphones. You can play on any device with a modern web browser.

How are game results saved?

Your best scores (moves and time) are stored directly in your browser using localStorage. This means your progress is saved on your device automatically.

Can I reset the leaderboard?

Yes. To clear your locally stored scores, open your browser's developer console and run the command: localStorage.removeItem('sl_leaderboard'). Then refresh the page.

What controls are supported?

Simply click or tap on the cards to flip them. The game is designed for intuitive mouse and touch controls. There are no complex keyboard commands required.

Is the game free to play?

Absolutely. Memory Match Master is completely free to play. We are committed to providing a fun and accessible brain-training experience for everyone without any cost.
